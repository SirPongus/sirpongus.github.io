Re-Volt

An R/C car racing game from 1999. The catalyst of my favourite online community. I did some stuff for it.

The New Are (of Shit)

After the creation of the Discord server in 2016, my wonderful friend and abominable snowman, Marv, went on to create what would later become Re-Volt I/O. While he dealt with the groundwork and all, you know, the important stuff, I was busy organising weekly online racing events that are still ongoing to this day. This helped revitalize the community, and I sure as hell am proud of it. It was the most notable thing I did for the community since a banana on wheels.

I really didn't do much else aside from that, but Marv's a rather humble guy and insists on not claiming all the credit, so let's pretend I actually contributed something worthwhile to the site itself, aight?

Custom Content

Every now and then, I decide to work on mods for the game. Usually, they end up as complete jokes. Can't help it, old habits die hard. Still, I do come up with proper creations every now and then, but I think it's pretty clear at this point that I excel at oversized bananas on wheels and realistic bricks with forks wedged into them.
